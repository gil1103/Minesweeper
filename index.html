Mine Sweeper

Functionality and Features

•Left click reveals the cell’s content

•Right click flags/unflags a suspected cell

Game ends when:

o LOSE: when clicking a mine, all mines should be revealed

o WIN: all the mines are flagged, and all the other cells are

cases :

o MINE – when a mine clicked, this mine revealed

o Cell with neighbors – reveal the cell alone

o Cell without neighbors – expand it and its 1st degree neighbors

Lives:
The user has 3 LIVES

When a MINE is clicked, The Lives counter decrease

Game restart:
by pressing the button at the top

Hints:
The player has 3 hints

When hint is active, it start blinking and at that time

the user can click an unrevealed cell to see the cell content

and its neighbors for 1 second, before it get back to previous state

High Scores:
Previous high Scores records per level are presented on the bottom left side

and can be reset by pressing the reset button

Safe click:
The player has 3 Safe-Clicks

Clicking the Safe-Click button will mark a covered cell (for a few seconds)

that is safe to click (does not contain a MINE)

Place Mines
A mode in which the player first positions the mines (by clicking cells)

and then plays

Undo
each click on that button takes the game back by one step (can go

all the way back to game start)
